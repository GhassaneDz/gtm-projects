1 Principles
============

  This projects illustrates the use of pattern strategy. It simulates a
  very simple game with different characters (a warrior and a
  priest). Their default behavior is, respectively, "walking and
  fighting with a gun", and "walking and refuse to fight".


2 How to use
============

  You will note the change in behavior in the simulation. This shows the
  possibility to attribute new behaviour to objects already
  instantiated.
